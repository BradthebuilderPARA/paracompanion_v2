# Privacy Policy

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how ParaCompanion Ltd (“Company”, “we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and protects personal data when you use the ParaCompanion platform (“Platform”).

We are committed to processing personal data:

* Lawfully, fairly, and transparently
* For specified and legitimate purposes
* In a way that ensures appropriate security

This Policy applies to all users of the Platform.

## 2. Data Controller

ParaCompanion Ltd is the **data controller** for the purposes of UK data protection law.

## 3. Scope of This Policy

This Policy applies to:

* Website users
* App users
* Registered account holders
* Individuals contacting us

It does **not** apply to third-party websites or services linked from the Platform.

## 4. Personal Data We Collect (Article 13)

We collect the following categories of personal data:

### 4.1 Account and Identity Data

* Name (if provided)
* Email address
* Account identifiers
* Role (e.g. student, practitioner)
* Trust or organisational affiliation (if provided)
* HCPC number (optional)

### 4.2 Subscription and Transaction Data

* Subscription tier
* Payment status
* Billing history (processed via third-party payment providers)

### 4.3 Usage and Technical Data

* Feature usage
* Interaction patterns
* Device type and operating system
* Log data (timestamps, session activity)

### 4.4 Communications Data

* Messages sent via contact forms
* Support enquiries
* Verification submissions (e.g. learner ID badge)

## 5. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do **not** collect or process:

* Patient-identifiable data
* Clinical records
* Special category health data relating to patients

Users are **expressly prohibited** from entering such data into the Platform.

Any such data entered is unauthorised and outside the intended use of the Platform.

## 6. How We Collect Data

We collect data:

### Directly from you

* When you register
* When you use the Platform
* When you contact us
* When you submit verification information

### Automatically

* Through cookies and similar technologies
* Through system logs and analytics

### From third parties (limited)

* Payment processors (e.g. subscription confirmation)
* Authentication providers (if used)

We do **not** obtain personal data from data brokers or external datasets.

## 7. Lawful Bases for Processing

We rely on the following lawful bases:

### Contractual Necessity

* To create and manage your account
* To provide Platform functionality
* To deliver subscription services

### Legitimate Interests

* To improve the Platform
* To maintain security and prevent misuse
* To analyse usage patterns
* To support product development

We ensure these interests do not override your rights.

### Legal Obligation

* To comply with HMRC requirements
* To respond to lawful requests from authorities

### Consent (where applicable)

* For optional communications (e.g. newsletters / cpd material)

You may withdraw consent at any time.

## 8. How We Use Your Data

We use personal data to:

* Provide and operate the Platform
* Authenticate users
* Deliver features (e.g. CPD tracking, My docs access)
* Process subscriptions
* Respond to enquiries
* Verify learner status
* Improve usability and performance
* Ensure security and prevent misuse

We do **not** use personal data for automated decision-making that produces legal or similarly significant effects.

## 9. Data Sharing

We do **not sell personal data**.

We may share data with:

### Service Providers

* Hosting providers
* Payment processors
* Email and communication services

These providers act as **data processors** under contractual safeguards.

### Legal and Regulatory Authorities

* Where required by law
* To enforce our Terms
* To protect legal rights

### Business Transfers

* In the event of merger, acquisition, or investment
* Subject to appropriate safeguards

## 10. International Transfers

Where data is transferred outside the UK:

* We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as:
  * UK International Data Transfer Agreements (IDTA)
  * Adequacy decisions

## 11. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary:

* **Account data:** While your account is active
* **Post-deletion:** Within a reasonable period after closure
* **Financial records:** In line with HMRC requirements
* **Verification data:** Retained only as long as necessary for audit purposes

We regularly review retention periods.

## 12. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures, including:

* Encryption (in transit and at rest where appropriate)
* Access controls and authentication
* Least-privilege access principles
* Monitoring and logging
* Regular security reviews

Despite this, no system is completely secure. Users share data at their own risk.

## 13. Your Rights

You have the right to:

* **Access** your personal data
* **Rectify** inaccurate data
* **Erase** your data (“right to be forgotten”)
* **Restrict processing**
* **Data portability**
* **Object to processing** (including legitimate interests)
* **Withdraw consent** where applicable

To exercise your rights, contact: admin via a web message or complete the contact form

Information Commissioner's Office
