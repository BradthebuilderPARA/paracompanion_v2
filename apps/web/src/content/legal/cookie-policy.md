# Cookie Policy

## 1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how ParaCompanion Ltd (“Company”, “we”, “us”) uses cookies and similar technologies on the ParaCompanion platform (“Platform”).

We use cookies only where necessary to:

* Operate the Platform securely
* Maintain user sessions
* Improve performance and reliability

We do **not** use cookies for advertising or data resale.

## 2. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you access a website or application.

They allow the Platform to:

* Recognise your device
* Maintain your session
* Store preferences
* Collect limited usage data

Similar technologies (e.g. local storage, SDK identifiers) may be used in mobile applications for the same purposes.

## 3. Types of Cookies We Use

### 3.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the operation of the Platform.

They include:

* Authentication and login session cookies
* Security and fraud prevention mechanisms
* Session persistence (“auto login”)
* Core system functionality

**Purpose:**

* Keep you logged in securely
* Prevent unauthorised access
* Ensure the Platform functions correctly

Without these cookies, the Platform cannot operate.

### 3.2 Performance and Analytics Cookies

We use limited analytics to understand how the Platform is used and to improve performance.

These cookies may collect:

* Page views and feature usage
* Device type and operating system
* Session duration
* Error and crash data

**Purpose:**

* Improve usability and performance
* Identify bugs and reliability issues
* Optimise user experience

**Important:**

* Data is aggregated where possible
* We do not use analytics for advertising
* We do not sell or share this data for commercial targeting

## 4. Legal Basis for Cookie Use

We rely on:

* **Legitimate interest** for strictly necessary cookies
* **Consent** for analytics and performance cookies

You will be asked to provide consent for non-essential cookies when you first use the Platform.

## 5. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can control cookies in several ways:

### Platform Controls

* Accept or reject analytics cookies via the cookie banner
* Update preferences in settings (if available)

### Browser Settings

You can block or delete cookies through your browser:

* Chrome, Safari, Edge, Firefox all provide controls

Note:
Blocking essential cookies may prevent the Platform from functioning correctly.

## 6. Mobile Applications

Where ParaCompanion is used as a mobile app:

* Equivalent technologies (e.g. secure storage, device identifiers) are used
* These serve the same purposes as cookies:
  * Authentication
  * Session persistence
  * Performance monitoring
